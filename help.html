How to Use Our Database

To begin your search of available classrooms at a specific time, or a wholesale search for any open rooms, you first need to select a building and/or a time.

In selecting only the time, you will get a broad and universal search of all available rooms at the chosen time. Selecting both a classroom and a time will allow for a fixed and specified time, for a club meeting or another particular activity.

Most common errors when utilising the directory is in the building name, time, or classroom ID. Make sure it is the correct time, including AM and PM. As well as correct IDs of rooms and/ or buildings.
